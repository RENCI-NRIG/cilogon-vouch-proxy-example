You should have noticed a Cookie being set after you've logged in using one of the above examples. Copy the Cookie to your clipboard before continuing to the next steps.

- Export your Cookie: $ export COOKIE='cilogon-example=H4sIAAAAAAAA_...__8aC4oEQCAAA'
- Make a non-authenticated curl call (no cookie): $ curl --insecure https://127.0.0.1:8443/app/flask
- Make an authenticated curl call (use cookie): $ curl --insecure --cookie $COOKIE https://127.0.0.1:8443/app/flask
